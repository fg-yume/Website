About

I'm Freddy Garcia, a student at Rochester Institute of Technology currently pursuing a B.S. in Game Design and Development. Although programming is my strong point when it comes to game development, I absolutely love the design phases of games. Listening to others' ideas as well as sharing my own has always been something that I find extremely enjoyable.

Highlight: Food Friends

Mobile controls are currently being worked on

Food Friends is a casual game built using Javascript and the canvas API. Food Friends was originally created for a game jam at Paderborn University. In order to keep true to the game jam's theme of "transatlantic friendship", Food Friends' core mechanic is to control various different countries into eating food that is not associated with their own country.
